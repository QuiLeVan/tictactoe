Gaming Privacy Policy

This statement discloses the privacy practices that apply to the online and mobile collection of data collected through ACFFG games, owned and operated by Awesome Cool and Free Fun Games LLC.

Awesome Cool and Free Fun Games LLC is strongly committed to protecting your privacy online.

Please read the following privacy policy to understand how your personal information will be treated. We may update this privacy policy periodically. Latest version will always be available at this page.

By accessing this page, you agree that you have read and understand this Privacy Policy, and that you accept and consent to the privacy practices described in this Privacy Policy.

Your Information is Secure

We respect and protect your privacy. Awesome Cool and Free Fun Games LLC is the sole owner of the information collected through registration to some of our gaming products. We only have access to or collect information that you voluntarily give us.

The personal information we collect may include your name or pseudonym, demographic information such as postal code, preferences, and interests, the version of phone or desktop operating system you used to access our products, Facebook unique user ID, etc.

We may use this information for internal record keeping, improving our products and services providing online score leaderboards, tracking your in-game achievements and for analytic purposes.

We will not share this information with anyone outside of our organization, other than as necessary to fulfill your request, e.g. to ship your order. It is our policy not to sell lists containing personal information about our users, registrants or subscribers.

Awesome Cool and Free Fun Games LLC also use personally identifiable information to complete transactions. Furthermore we use personally identifiable information to inform you of services, products and promotions that we, based on the information submitted to us by you, think will be of interest to you or your organization.
From time to time we may send you communications regarding our products and services.
You may at any time opt-out from receiving electronic communications as explained below.
Your e-mail will not be shared with one outside Awesome Cool and Free Fun Games LLC.
You can remove your e-mail address from our mailing list by clicking on the automatic “unsubscribe” link in any of our mailings, simply at unsubscribe email.
